Description

This meal originates from Western Africa and particulary from the Hausa people in Northern Nigeria but it is common eated by virtually everyone and as a snack

Ingredients

  • Peppered grounded groundnut
  • Vegetable oil
  • Pepper
  • Protein Source usally beaf

Steps

  1. The beaf is first cut very thin after which it is marinated
  2. The beaf is oiled and roasted
  3. The peppered grounded groundnut is applied on the cooled oiled beef until it stick to the surface
  4. This is then roasted again and served
