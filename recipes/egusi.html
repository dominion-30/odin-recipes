Egusi and Pounded Yam

Visit Home for more recipes

Description

This meal originates from Western Africa and particulary from the Yoruba people in South Western Nigeria

Ingredients

  • Egusi
  • Vegetable leaf
  • Pepper
  • Protein Source
  • Yam

Steps

  1. Make the soup by adding grounded melon into a pot with oil and stir till desired
  2. Peel and boil yam
  3. Pound boiled yam into a solid but tender form
  4. Serve in a dip bowl or multiple plates
